## Key finding: this chapter has no semester-by-semester degree curriculum at all

All 3 pages of this chapter (printed pp. 133, 134, 135 — raw PDF pages 132, 133, 134) were
rendered at 2–3x resolution and visually inspected in full, and cross-checked against the
plain-text extraction. The chapter contains:

1. **p. 133** — Department roster (head of department, deputy, faculty, adjunct lecturers,
   lab manager) and the opening of "תיאור המחלקה" (department description).
2. **p. 134** — Continuation of the department description: teaching philosophy for physics
   service courses, list of teaching goals, and general remarks about labs and workshops.
3. **p. 135** — "רשימת קורסי הפיזיקה" (List of Physics Courses) — a **flat table** of physics
   courses the department teaches *as service courses to other engineering departments*
   (see full table below).

**There is no "סמסטר 1" … "סמסטר 8" course-table sequence anywhere in this chapter**, and
consequently **no "קורסי קדם וקורסים צמודים" (prerequisite/corequisite) column exists to
inspect for underline formatting**. The physics-course-list table on p. 135 has a completely
different column set: מס' הקורס (course number), שם הקורס (course name), ה/ת/מ (weekly
lecture/tutorial/lab hours), נ"ז (credits), and הקורס ניתן למחלקה (which department the course
is taught to) — there is no prerequisite column at all, so no underline/corequisite
distinction applies here either.

The department's opening description (p. 133) states it is "אחראית ... על תוכנית הלימודים
בהנדסה אופטית" (also responsible for the optical-engineering course of study), but no
follow-on semester tables for such a program appear anywhere in this chapter, and a full-text
search of the plain-text twin for the exact phrase "פיזיקה והנדסה אופטית" across all 16,450
lines returns only two hits: the table-of-contents entry (line 120) and the department-title
line (line 8581) — no third occurrence introducing a curriculum table elsewhere in the
document. A broader search for "אופטית" across all 251 rendered pages returns hits only on:
pp. 3, 20, 98, 99 (front matter / TOC), 133–134 (this chapter — a research-topics mention,
"חישה ביולוגית אופטית" = "optical biological sensing", not a course table), and p. 209 (an
M.Sc. Systems Engineering admissions page that generically lists "הנדסה אופטית" alongside
"הנדסה כימית" — Chemical Engineering, which Braude does not offer as a B.Sc. either — as one
of several *accepted prior-degree fields* for applicants from any institution, not a
Braude-specific program reference).

**Conclusion: this program, as represented in this yearbook edition, does not have its own
mandatory (חובה) or elective (בחירה) semester-by-semester course structure.** It is a
service-teaching department only. Nothing was fabricated or inferred to fill this gap; the
absence is reported as the actual finding.
