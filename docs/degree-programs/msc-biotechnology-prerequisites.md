Chapter: פרק ז' – לימודים לתואר מוסמך במדעים M.Sc. → תואר שני בביוטכנולוגיה (TOC page 199).

## Key finding: no prerequisite/corequisite column in this program's tables

Unlike the B.Sc. program tables (which all carry a "קורסי קדם וקורסים צמודים" — prerequisite/
corequisite — column), the M.Sc. Biotechnology course tables were rendered and visually
inspected page by page (printed pp. 200, 201, 202) and **do not contain a prerequisite/
corequisite column at all**. Each table lists only two columns: מספר קורס (course number) and
שם הקורס (course name) — no credit column, no teaching-hours column, and no prerequisite
column. No course code or course name anywhere in this chapter is rendered with underline
formatting (the document's convention for marking a corequisite/"קורס צמוד"), because there is
no prerequisite/corequisite column to underline within.

This was confirmed by:
1. Full-text search of the plain-text twin for "קורסי קדם" (the column header used everywhere
   in the B.Sc. chapters) — zero matches inside the biotechnology M.Sc. chapter (txt lines
   ~13177–13392).
2. Rendering PDF pages 199–202 (0-indexed 198–201) at 3x resolution and visually inspecting
   every table — confirmed only "מספר קורס" / "שם הקורס" columns, no underlined course codes.

Consequently, **no strict-prerequisite vs. corequisite distinction could be extracted for this
program**, because the source document does not encode one for these tables. This is
reported as the actual (empty) finding rather than inferred or fabricated.

   ומעלה; או תואר ראשון )שלוש שנתי( במדעי החיים/ כימיה.
   (A four-year B.Sc. in biotechnology/chemical/biomedical engineering with a weighted average
   of 78+ is required; or a three-year B.Sc. in life sciences/chemistry.)
2. ניתן להשלים קורסים חסרים במחלקה להנדסת ביוטכנולוגיה לפני או במהלך הסמסטר הראשון
   ללימודי התואר השני.
   (Missing courses may be completed in the Biotechnology Engineering department before or
   during the first semester of the M.Sc.)
3. רשימת הקורסים מתואר ראשון הנדרשים לתוכנית, מפורטת בהמשך הפרק.
   (The list of B.Sc.-level courses required for the program is detailed later in the chapter —
   this is the list reproduced above.)

Degree award: בתום לימודיו יקבל הסטודנט תואר שני נלמד )M.Sc.( בביוטכנולוגיה. לשם קבלתו על
הסטודנט לצבור 45 נ"ז.
(Upon completion the student receives an M.Sc. in Biotechnology; 45 credits required.)

  workshop courses; remaining slots are open "קורס בחירה" electives without fixed codes) —
  see tables above for the exact 14 rows including electives placeholders.
- Undergraduate prerequisite (admission) courses listed: 17 courses across 4 subject groups.
- Corequisite ("קורס צמוד", underlined) pairs found: **0** — this program's tables do not carry
  a prerequisite/corequisite column, so no strict-vs-corequisite distinction exists to extract.
